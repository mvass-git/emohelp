About Us

What is Emohelp?

Emohelp is a free website with a database of categorized materials and resources related to common critical conditions and issues.

Here you can find support for what concerns or interests you, receiving recommendations in the form of links to articles, apps, movies, books, and even music.

Our goal is to create a space where you can feel more comfortable with your problems and learn more about them through the collected materials.

To determine your current most pressing condition, the site offers a short test.

You can also browse the catalog and choose what interests you without taking the test.

You can take the test multiple times.

Is it a professional mental help website?

Emohelp does not provide professional psychological assistance; it is only a resource for identifying your problematic states and providing materials from pop culture.

The test result is not a diagnosis.

For professional help, please consult a specialist.

About the test

The test consists of 20 questions.

It is based on official questionnaires in the categories of Loneliness, Existential, Depressive, Social Connectedness, Motivation.

Do not interpret the test results as a diagnosis.

The test can be taken as many times as desired.

You may also choose not to take it at all.
